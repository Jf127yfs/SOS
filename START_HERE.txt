🎭 PYRAMID STRUCTURE
   • 1 focal dancer (team leader)
   • 2 core members (60-80% match, close compatibility)
   • 3 wild cards (30-50% match, diversity/growth)
   • Total: 6 dancers per team

🏆 AUTO-GENERATED TEAM NAMES
   Genre-based:
   • Pop → "Pop Perfection", "Pop Dynasty", "Chart Toppers"
   • R&B → "R&B Royalty", "Smooth Operators", "Soul Squad"
   • Indie/Alt → "Indie Icons", "Alt Elite", "Underground Kings"
   
   Interest-based:
   • Music → "Harmony Squad", "Rhythm Rebels"
   • Fitness → "Flex Force", "Gym Legends"
   • Gaming → "Level Up Crew", "Game Changers"
   
   30+ total variations!

💬 ABBY LEE COMMENTARY

   • Checks column 30 (PHOTO_URL_COL) in Form Responses
   • Falls back to Abby Lee placeholder if missing

🎵 GENRE MIXING
   • Algorithm prioritizes different music genres
   • Bonus for diversity: +0.3 similarity for different genres
   • Result: Teams with 3-5 different genres

🔄 REPEATS ALLOWED
   • Dancers can appear on multiple teams
   • But never as focal twice in same batch
   • Ensures everyone gets featured
